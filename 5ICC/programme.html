Programme and Abstracts of Talks

Programme Overview

  • Sunday 3rd December: welcome reception
  • Monday 4th December: talks
  • Tuesday 5th December: talks, public lecture in the evening
  • Wednesday 6th December: talks in the morning, excursion in the afternoon
  • Thursday 7th December: talks
  • Friday 8th December: talks, conference dinner in the evening
  • Saturday 9th December: talks in the morning

Welcome Party

There is a welcome party at 4-7pm on Sunday 3rd December in the ground floor of the maths building (9 Rainforest Walk, in the northwest corner of Monash campus).

Registration will also be open at this event, so you can collect your conference paperwork here. The materials will be provided in a zippable folder (but there will be no conference bag as such; please bring your own backpack if needed).

If you are unable to attend the welcome function, it will be possible to complete registration on Monday morning before the first talk.

Excursion

The conference excursion will take place on Wednesday afternoon.

  • Option 1: Lunch, followed by a visit to Healesville Sanctuary, where you can see many of Australia's unique animals including kangaroos, koalas, playtpus, echidnas etc.
  • Option 2: Lunch, followed by wine tasting at several wineries in the beautiful Yarra valley.

Each conference participant should have received an email asking them to select one of these options. If you have not submitted an option from the email, then please do so. If you have not received this email, please contact the organisers.

Survivors' party

A survivors' party will be hosted by Nick and Hania Wormald from 8pm on Saturday 9th December at 4 Bishop Court, Mt Waverley (ph 8802 7533).
