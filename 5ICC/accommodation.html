Mannix College

Mannix College is the main venue for conference accommodation. It is next to the Clayton campus of Monash University, where the conference will be held. It is also close to the bus station, for transport into Melbourne city. Here is the campus map.

The following discounted rates are available for conference attendees:

  • single occupancy hotel-style room in Gryphon House, $110/night including meals
  • double occupancy hotel-style room in Gryphon House, $140/night including meals
  • student room with ensuite, $85/night or $105/night including meals
  • student room with shared bathroom, $77/night or $97/night including meals

There are a limited number of hotel-style rooms and rooms with ensuite. They are available on a first-come, first-served basis.

To book rooms at Mannix College, email enquiries.mannix@monash.edu or phone +61 3 9905 0990. Mention you are attending 5ICC to get the discounted rate.

Normanby House

Normanby House is on the north side of the Monash Clayton campus. It offers studio apartments for $77.85/night or 2-bedroom apartments for $88.75/night. For bookings phone +61 3 9905 6266 or email mrs.applications@monash.edu.
