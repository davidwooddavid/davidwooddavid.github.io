Prospective students

If you are interested in me supervising your Ph.D. studies, then read "How to choose a good supervisor" and read my "School postgraduate entry guidelines", then send me an email including:

  • your full CV and official academic transcripts,
  • a copy of any theses or research papers written,
  • a detailed statement of why you want me to be your supervisor, focusing on specific research interests in common, and
  • a statement about how you will pay the university fees, and in particular, whether you will need a scholarship.

If you have publications in any predatory journals (typically those with author-paid fees), then it is unlikely I will consider supervising you. I do not have funds to support interns or visiting undergraduate students, so do not expect a reply to such requests.
