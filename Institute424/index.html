This research-intensive workshop will be held at Monash University in Melbourne on 28 November - 2 December 2022. Mathematicians from across the globe will come together to work on open problems in graph minors, graph product structure theory, and probabilistic combinatorics. All the time is allocated to collaborative research. The goal is to create an environment where mathematicians at all career stages work side-by-side. We anticipate that open problems will be solved, and lasting collaborations will be initiated.
